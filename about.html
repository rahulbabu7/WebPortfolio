About Me

Hello!

I'm Rahul Babu, a passionate front-end developer based in Palai, Kerala. I'm currently in my final year of Computer Science Engineering at St. Joseph's College of Engineering.

With over 3 years of education in computer science and engineering, I have developed strong skills in front-end web development, specializing in Vue.js. I find great joy in creating intuitive user experiences and visually appealing interfaces.

DAKSHA - A digital platform aimed at providing education to underprivileged children in Kerala. I worked on the front-end of this non-profit platform.
